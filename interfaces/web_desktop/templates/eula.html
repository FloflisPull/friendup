Friend Sky Code of Conduct

Preamble

Friend Sky is an online community and collaboration service that is hosting different and unique users from all over the world. It is the first Sky Computing network that is built using the Friend platform. To enable friendly interactions and a truly vibrant community, we have authored this document to set some basic standards.

This Code of Conduct is shared by all contributors and users who engage with the Friend Sky community in any of the collaborative services. By breaking the Code of Conduct, Friend Sky staff may issue a warning and in a worst case scenario block your account.

Overview

This Code of Conduct presents a summary of the shared values and “common sense” thinking in our community. The basic social ingredients that hold our project together include:

  • Be friendly
  • Be respectful
  • Be friendly

Being friendly is in the name of Friend. We strive to create a community where people feel welcome and where they are appreciated by others. Make sure to give people a chance. Do not answer messages too harshly or confront other users with personal attacks. In Friend Sky, we will not accept any form of personal harassment.

Be respectful

The community found on Friend Sky is diverse and from many different places all over the world. Be mindful of your language and appreciate that people may interpret your messages differently based on their unique cultural context.
